About Teller's Account

The job of the Meter Reader is simply to input reading no more, no less

Input Reading / Bills Payment / Add Consumer to Database

STEP 1 - Input Reading
  • Click Meter Reading (As shown)

STEP 2 - Input Reading
  • Once youve done that, you will see the consumer list (As shown above)
  • To input reading, lets use a sample(See image above)
  • Click Input Reading the green button(As shown)

STEP 3 - Input Reading
  • Inside the sample Account, you will see a lot of options like input reading, modify, and you can also input reading in the past month as you wish(As shown above)
  • To input the latest reading, look at the current month and you'll see a blank space and input the reading and hit commit(See image above)
  • To input the past reading, you can see the past months with reading and you can change reading and hit modify to apply the changes of the past reading(See image above)
  • After you have inputted the reading, be it the past or the latest, go to the modify meter reading option to confirm or approve the changes

STEP 1 Bills Payments
  • To process Bills Payments, click Bills Payment Option in the navigation(As shown)
  • Lets use a consumer as sample, click the green button(see image above)

STEP 2 - Bills Payment
  • You can see the information inside the consumer account(As shown)
  • To pay, click Pay Button(see image above)

STEP 3 - Bills Payment
  • Upon hitting the Pay Button, a dialog box will pop up (As shown)
  • You can input the amount to pay and OR number and hit submit(see image above)
  • Congrats you have made payment!(As shown)

STEP 4 - Bills Payment / Transaction History
  • You can view the transaction history by simply scrolling down and hit payment history button (As shown)

STEP 5 - Bills Payment / Transaction History
  • As you can see, every transactions are recorded(As shown)
  • You can see what time, date, amount, and the one who received the payment(see image above)
