About Engineer's Account

The Engineer's Account is a vital piece of the chain of Application Approvals its the second piece of the puzzle. Can View Reports and Rates Management. The only vital task is to approve application requests from the checker.

Approve Application Request / View Reports and Rates Management
  • Log-in as Engineer
  • Click Application Request to see requests of approval from the engineer(As shown)
  • Click View and scroll down and approve or decline the application request(As shown)
  • Click Approve and go to the navigation bar and click Approved Applicants and you'll see the application you approved(As shown)
  • Click view reports to view 3 types of reports such as (Usage Reports, Revenue Reports, and Unsettled Bills) and lastly click Rates Management Option (it can be seen in the Navigation bar) to view Rates of Services(As shown)

STEP 1 - Approve Applicants
  • Welcome to the Engineer's Account dashboard
  • In the dashboard, there are three options, Reports, Approve Application, Rates Management, and Account Settings
  • So for now i'll show you how to Approve Applicants
  • Click Approve Application(in the navigation bar)

STEP 2 - Approve Applicants
  • After Clicking Approve Application, click Application Request.
  • By doing so, you'll see the list of consumer accounts that are yet to be approved by you sent by the checker.
  • But for now you only have a single request by the Checker to be approved by you so lets use that as a Sample.
  • To Approve the Request,Click view (as pointed by the purple arrow)
  • After clicking view, you will be prompted to the insides or page of the application request

STEP 3 - Approve Applicants
  • So this is the information containing the request
  • You can see the information of the application Name, Address, etc.
  • To Approve, scroll down and click Approve

STEP 4 - Approve Applicants
  • When you reach at the bottom, click approve to approve
  • After that click the back button(you can see it in the navigation bar)
  • And click Approved Applicants option.

View Usage Reports
  • To View Usage Reports, Click Usage Reports(see image above)
  • By clicking Usage Reports, you'll see The Usage Reports of usage per month, year and Barangay

View Revenue Reports
  • To View Revenue Reports, Click Revenue Reports(see image above)
  • By clicking Revenue Reports, you'll see The Collectibles/Collection per year, month, and barangay

View Unsettled Bills
  • To View Unsettled Bills, Click Unsettled Bills(see image above)
  • By clicking Unsettled Bills option, you'll see The list of Consumers yet to pay or unpaid bills and you can view their account by clicking view.
  • To view their account, click view and you'll see the bills unpaid and you can choose what year

View Rates Management
  • To View Rates Management, Click Rates Management(see image above)
  • By clicking Rates Management, you'll be able to view The Rates of Services

My Account
